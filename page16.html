Have a Technical Detox

  1. Turn your phone on do not disturb. Most devices have a setting where you can lock it for a specific amount of time. Your texts and alerts will be saved and sent to you once you turn off the do not disturb setting. Try that for 10 minutes one day, 15 minutes the next, increase your time by 5 minutes each day until you’re comfortable enough to detach for 1 hour or more!

  2. Ask a friend to hold your phone next time you’re out together. Don’t let them give it to you until you part ways. It gives your friend the quality time and appreciation they deserve!

  3. Relax! Press play on your favorite Pandora mix. Grab a speaker or your headphones and sit outside. Test yourself and see if you can go without touching your phone for 1 song, then 2, maybe three!

  4. Go on a hike. Pretty sure there’s no wifi, but there will be some amazing views and nature to take in! Psst! Use your phone to take some amazing Insta pics for posting later.
