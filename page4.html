Google Job Search

  • Let Google do the work for you

  • Save time with their search filters

  • Alerts about jobs bring the results to you

  • You are allowed to be picky

Upon a Deeper Look

  1. Google Job Search is basically TripAdvisor for jobs. Take advantage of their super easy search tool.

  2. Filter! At the very top of Google Search you’ll see a search bar, enter a location or your computer will choose one for you based on your IP address. From there you can literally set up filters for a company you’d like to look at, how far you want to be from your location (or if you’d even like to work from home), the type of company you’d like to work for, even the title you’d love to have. Once your filters are set, BOOM! Google brings you a list of jobs available based on your filters.

  3. Let Google be your recruiter, by setting up an alert for your job search. The alert will save all of your filters and send you an email any time a new job is posted that fits your requirements. Honestly, what’s better than that?

Tips

Looking for a job can be draining on your confidence and energy. Let Google do the work for you, by using Google Job Search the right way can help you land your dream job faster. Now all you have to do is get ready for your interviews (eek!); I guess you’ll need a wardrobe refresh too!
