Upon a Deeper Look

  1. It’s always the volunteer time of the year.Consider volunteering at the Henry Ford Allegiance hospital.

  2. Fill out an application.

  3. Go online and print the application. Run a background check and do paperwork required. https://www.henryford.com/-/media/files/henry-ford/locations/allegiance/hfah-volunteer-application-2019.pdf

  4. Next, you will come in an do an interview and see what your schedule looks like.

  5. Shifts are from noon to four.

  6. Choose which jobs you can handle like in the gift shop, emergency department, escorting people around and guiding them throughout the hospital.
